Moneypoly

Financial Decisions As Gameplay

Developing Financial Literacy

  • Target Audience: Young adults starting to manage their own finances
  • Finance is often intimidating, complex, and full of jargon
  • Real-world mistakes carry heavy and stressful consequences
  • Traditional learning methods lack practical engagement

Our Solution: A Financial Sandbox

  • Gamifying Financial Decisions
  • Experiential learning through engaging multiplayer strategy
  • A risk-free environment to test
    financial strategies
  • No random dice rolls – every move
    is intentional
  • Every turn is a money decision: invest, borrow, and react to market news.

Mastering the Markets

  • Learn to navigate simulated price histories and market regimes
  • Trade traditional stocks to build slow, stable wealth and receive dividends
  • Explore cryptocurrencies for
    high-risk rewards
  • Hands-on experience managing market volatility

Strategic Banking Tools

  • Take credits to strategically leverage lucrative trades
  • Use deposits to build guaranteed
    steady yields
  • Buy insurance against potential personal financial disasters
  • Understand the compound impact of
    term lengths

Adapting to Unpredictability

  • Face macro-economic shocks like random fuel price spikes
  • Navigate sector crashes and unexpected tax burdens
  • Learn to maintain healthy liquidity for emergencies
  • Test and adapt long-term strategies under pressure

The Vision & Impact

  • Empower young people with a strong wealth-building mindset
  • Translate abstract finance concepts into actionable insights
  • Make financial literacy an engaging, social group experience
  • Prepare players for their independent financial futures
